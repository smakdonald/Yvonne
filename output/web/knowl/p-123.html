Walk through chart together. Say that although these are things that may be familiar, we are going over them so we are all on the same page with each other. As well, this way of organizing our thoughts will be useful when we get to other kinds of powers. Be sure that teachers notice how representations of addition and multiplication map to analogous representations of multiplication and exponentiation respectively. Namely, pictures representing ones as boxes and repeated addition map nicely to the as and repeated multiplication.
