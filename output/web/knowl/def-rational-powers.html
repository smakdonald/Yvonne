Definition 1.24. Rational Powers: Draft.

When \(p/q \in \mathbb{Q}^+\text{,}\) where \(p, q \in \mathbb{N}^+\text{,}\) we define
  • \(a^{1/q}\) as the positive number that you multiply \(q\) times to get \(a\text{.}\)Equivalently, it is a positive or zero solution to \(x^q=a\text{.}\) This is also known as a .
  • \(a^{p/q}\) as \((a^{1/q})^p\text{,}\) which we take to be equivalent to \((a^p)^{1/q}\text{.}\)
