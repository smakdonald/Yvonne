Suppose that you are teaching about real powers, and you use \(5^\pi\) as an example. A student asks you about power properties, and you review them using the example:
\begin{equation*} 5^{3.1}=5^{3+0.1}=5^{3}5^{0.1}. \end{equation*}
You ask them to break down \(5^{3.141}\) in a similar fashion.
