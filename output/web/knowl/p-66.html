Be sure the following things come out in the discussion:
  • “Tint” means window tint. The higher the tint, the darker things appear.
  • p% tint: The sunglasses reduce visible light transmission by p%.
  • With 50% tint glasses, you see 50% of the light.
  • With 60% tint glasses, you see 40% of the light.
