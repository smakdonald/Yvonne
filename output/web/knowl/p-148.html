  • “\(0\)” is the movement of staying still, and its location is the origin.
  • “\(1\)” is the movement of translating \(1\) unit to the right, and the location of moving \(1\) unit to the right when starting from \(0\text{.}\)
  • “\(n\)”, for \(n>0\text{,}\) is the movement of \(n\) repeated translations of \(1\text{,}\) and the location of moving \(n\) units to the right when starting from \(0\text{.}\)
